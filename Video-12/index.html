BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool that helps individuals estimate their body fat based on their height and weight. It provides a simple numeric result that can indicate whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. The formula used to calculate BMI is:

BMI is widely used in healthcare to assess an individual's health status and identify potential weight-related issues. While it's a useful guideline, it doesn't directly measure body fat or take into account muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.

A BMI calculator can be found in many health apps, websites, or created using simple programming tools like Python or JavaScript. It's an essential and easy-to-use feature for anyone wanting to track their health and fitness journey.
