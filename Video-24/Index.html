Python Lists

In this lecture, you'll learn about lists in Python, their features, and how to perform common operations with them.

  • Lists are ordered, mutable collections of items.
  • They can store elements of different data types.
  • Lists support indexing, slicing, and various built-in methods.
  • You can add, remove, and modify elements in a list.
  • Lists are defined using square brackets: my_list = [1, 2, 3]
